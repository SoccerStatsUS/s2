About

The goal of soccerstats.us is to create a free and complete resource for anyone interested in US soccer.

That effort currently focuses on compiling, organizing, and clearly presenting American soccer statistics and data from the very beginnings of the sport in the United States to the present day.

We hope to help people better understand the rich and fascinating history of the most international sport in the context of the world's most international country.
