This site aims to provide comprehensive statistics for all soccer games played in the United States since 1885.

The site can be seen to cover a variety of competitions - league competitions, cup competitions, domestic international competitions, national team competitions, and friendly competitions.

Notable league competitions include:

  • American League of Professional Football of 1894, the first american soccer league.
  • American Soccer League of 1922-1931, a major international league of the time
  • American Soccer League of 1934-1983, a much smaller league.
  • North American Soccer League of 1968-1984, featuring Pele, professional soccer's rebirth in America.
  • Major League Soccer of 1996 to today, the current American first division.

Also of interest are the USL First division, USL Second Division, Western Soccer Alliance, American Professional Soccer League, St. Louis Soccer League, National Association Football League, and Major Indoor Soccer League.

Notable cup competitions include:

  • American Cup, the first national American cup competition, started in ?
  • Lamar Hunt U.S. Open Cup, the current national cup competition, started in ? as a competitor to the American Cup.
  • Lewis Cup, a league cup competition played itermittently by both incarnations of the American Soccer League.
  • NASL Playoffs, the playoffs for the NASL.
  • MLS Cup Playoffs, the playoffs for the MLS.

Also fo interest are the Professional Cup, US Professional Cup, APSL Playoffs, WSA Playoffs.
