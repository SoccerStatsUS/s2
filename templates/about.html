About

  • My goal for this website is to create a comprehensive statistical and informational resource for American soccer dating back to the football game played by the Oneida Soccer Club.
  • The website is open source. The source code can be found on github. I am happy to share any data upon request, and plan to improve data export on the site.
  • The site is currently in pre-release alpha. Data is not guaranteed to be accurate or complete.

Tour

Notable league competitions include:

  • American League of Professional Football of 1894, the first american soccer league, started by baseball owners, did make it through its first season.
  • American Soccer League of 1922-1931, a major international league of the time, flourished in Southern New England, New York, and Philadelphia.
  • American Soccer League of 1934-1983, a long-lasting semi-pro league, based only on the East Coast until the 1970's.
  • North American Soccer League of 1968-1984, the home of Pele, featured a host of notable international players, but ultimately failed due to always shaky financials.
  • Major League Soccer of 1996 to today, the current American first division.

Interesting minor leagues include: USL First division, USL Second Division, Western Soccer Alliance, American Professional Soccer League, St. Louis Soccer League, National Association Football League, and Major Indoor Soccer League.

Notable cup competitions include:

  • Lamar Hunt U.S. Open Cup, the most storied competition in US soccer history. Started in 1914 as the National Challenge Cup as a competitior to the American Cup.
  • American Cup, the first "national" American cup competition, started in 1885, only teams from the East coast competed.
  • Lewis Cup, a league cup competition played itermittently by both incarnations of the American Soccer League.
  • NASL Playoffs, the playoffs for the NASL.
  • MLS Cup Playoffs, the playoffs for the MLS.
