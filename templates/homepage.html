Welcome to the site. The goal here is to have comprehensive statistics for all soccer leagues and competitions in the history of the United States, beginning with the American League of Professional Football of 1894, through Major League Soccer, the new NASL and everything in between.

The site is currently under active development, so please keep in mind that none of the information is authoratative, and pages may break at any time.

Some of the more interesting items on the site include the original American Soccer League, a surprisingly strong American league that existed in the 1920's and 1930's, the NASL, which featured Pele, Franz Beckenbauer, Johan Cruyff, Hugo Sanchez and many other notable players played in from 1968 to 1984.

Also worth checking out are the Western Soccer Alliance and the American Professional Soccer League, which cropped up after the demise of the NASL and featured a bunch of future United States national team players like Eric Wynalda, Marcelo Balboa, and Tab Ramos.

Feel free to contact me at chris at socceroutsider.com with any questions, requests or bugs; please (please) definitely contact me if you have any data that you want included on the site.
